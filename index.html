Our story

Where tradition meets excellence.

Basbaas brings the bold, aromatic flavours of the Horn of Africa to South East London. Every dish is made fresh, from scratch, using exceptional ingredients and recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're new to Somali cuisine or a longtime fan, our table is set for you. Dine in, savour the flavour, and come back for more.
